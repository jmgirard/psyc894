Learning Outcomes

  1. Learn fundamental concepts underlying multilevel modeling

  2. Learn practical skills for implementing such models in R

  3. Instill a sense of mastery, curiosity, and fun

What you will learn here

  • When and why MLM is needed

  • Prepare a dataset for MLM

  • Estimate two-level models

  • Interpret random effects

  • Exposure to advanced topics

What you won’t learn here

  • Advanced longitudinal models

  • Advanced nested (3+ level) models

  • Advanced cross-classified models

  • Advanced Bayesian model estimation

  • Extensions (e.g., GLMM, MSEM, GAMMs)

Course Format

Lectures (1-2/week, 19 total)

  • Explaining concepts and demonstrating skills
  • Each is tied to one or more readings

Computer Labs (0-1/week, 8 total)

  • Six multi-part questions to apply your new skills
  • Demonstrate your understanding in controlled conditions

Final Project (1 week/semester, 2 total)

  • Apply your new skills to real-world data you care about
  • Challenge yourself to learn new things and specialize
